FAQ

Common questions.

What counts as a cleaning credit?

One standard item or cleaning area up to 10 ft × 10 ft. Specialty services like solar panels may require multiple credits depending on size and access.

Do I need to be home?

No. As long as the item is outside and water/electric access is available.

Can I change items month to month?

Yes. Your credits are flexible, so you can choose a different eligible item or service each month.

Are driveways included?

Full driveways are not included in the standard membership. Small concrete areas up to 10 ft × 10 ft may qualify as one credit.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly plans are designed to be simple and flexible.

What do I need to provide?

A standard water spigot, standard electrical outlet, and safe access to the item.
